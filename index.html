I am interested in the semi-automated verification and construction of programs and proofs. These two areas are related by the concept of correctness: verification attempts to prove that an existing artifact is what it purports to be, and construction attempts to build a ratified artifact from the ground up. Both cases are preeminently applicable in the engineering of safety and security critical software, as well as in formal mathematics.
